Hello!

I work on deployable and interpretable solutions for data-intensive research problems.

In my thesis research, I developed a human-in-the-loop framework and related methods that are used daily by public health stakeholders to identify and diagnose anomalies from large volumes of public health data.

My expertise is in applied AI, data science, and computer systems. I have extensive experience working directly with line-level and aggregated public health data.
